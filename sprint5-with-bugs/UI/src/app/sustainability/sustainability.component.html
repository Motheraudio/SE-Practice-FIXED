Sustainability & ESG

Our ESG Mission Statement

At Toolshop, we are committed to operating as a responsible and sustainable business. Our Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) framework guides every decision we make — from the products we source to the communities we serve.

We believe that business growth and environmental stewardship are not mutually exclusive. Our goal is to build a company that future generations can be proud of, by reducing our ecological footprint, championing fair labour practices, and maintaining the highest standards of corporate governance.

Environmental Commitment

We are actively working to reduce carbon emissions across our supply chain. Our initiatives include partnering with eco-certified suppliers, minimising packaging waste, and investing in energy-efficient logistics. By 2030, we aim to achieve net-zero carbon emissions in our direct operations.

  • Sourcing tools from suppliers with verified environmental certifications
  • Using recyclable and biodegradable packaging materials
  • Offsetting unavoidable emissions through verified carbon offset programmes
  • Measuring and reporting our annual carbon footprint

Social Responsibility

People are at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to fair and inclusive employment practices, safe working conditions, and active community engagement. Toolshop supports local tradespeople and small businesses by offering accessible pricing and dedicated B2B support.

  • Living wage commitment for all direct employees and contractors
  • Zero-tolerance policy for forced or child labour throughout our supply chain
  • Annual charitable donations to trade education and apprenticeship programmes
  • Diversity and inclusion targets reviewed by our board each quarter

Governance

Strong governance ensures our ESG commitments are more than words on a page. Our ESG steering committee meets quarterly to review progress against targets and report transparently to stakeholders.

  • Independent ESG audit conducted annually by a third-party assessor
  • ESG performance metrics published in our annual sustainability report
  • Whistleblower policy protecting employees who raise ethical concerns
  • Anti-corruption and anti-bribery training mandatory for all staff

Company Vision

Toolshop's vision is to be the most trusted and sustainable tooling partner for professionals and enthusiasts alike. We see a future where every tool sold contributes positively to the environment and society — and we are building that future, one transaction at a time.

Legal & Compliance

Toolshop complies with all applicable environmental regulations and reporting obligations in the jurisdictions where we operate. We adhere to internationally recognised frameworks including the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and the Global Reporting Initiative (GRI) standards.
